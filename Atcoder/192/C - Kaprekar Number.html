問題文

  • f(x)=g1(x)−g2(x)f(x)=g1(x)−g2(x)

Problem Statement

For an integer xx not less than 00, we define g1(x),g2(x),f(x)g1(x),g2(x),f(x) as follows:

  • g1(x)=g1(x)= the integer obtained by rearranging the digits in the decimal notation of xx in descending order
